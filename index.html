How Festeeve works

Upload your music to digital stores like Spotify and Apple Music in no time. Pay an annual fee and get unlimited distribution. No small letters. You get to keep 100% of your profit and distribute as many songs as you want for a fixed price.

Your Rights, always

Keep 100% of your rights and 100% of your royalties when uploading with us. We only charge an annual subscription fee for unlimited distribution. No cut from your profits.

Payout

As soon as you've earned USD 50, you can withdraw your royalties via wire transfer, whenever you want. The payout requirement is in place to reduce the amount of transaction fees that would dilute your income.
